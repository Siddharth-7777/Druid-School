what's new

  • Foreign training for Druid Obelix for a year in hunting porcupines

    The management has decided to have training for their teachers to multiply their talents. In that direction, Druid Obelix will be sent to Timbuctoo for a year to learn porcupine hunting. He will learn under another famous druid, namely, Druid Huntallix. Huntallix is in exile in Timbuctoo under the order of Druid Vitalstatistix. The Timbuctoo government has asked the services of Druid Obelix in reducing the menace of these giant rodents. He will bring back the rich knowledge acquired there in Timbuctoo.

  • A new brach of druid talent, "Fresh fried fish" will be introduced next year

    Druidess Impedimenta is already "on the job training" at an island near the Bermuda Triangle. There, she will learn the culinary art of cooking with fish from nobody less than Druidess Promfreta. She will bring back the rich culinary art of "fresh fried fish" to the Gaulish village. Already, a new kitchen is under construction under the guidance of Druid Unhygienix who has also taken the help of his father, Druid Unhealthix. The Gaulish village is eagerly waiting for the return of Druidess Impedimenta with empty stomachs. She is going to transfer her rich knowledge to her students in the coming years.
